Connect ntfy.sh
ntfy is a free, open-source push notification service. Install the app, enter a topic name below, and you're done — no account, no credit card, no expiry.
1
Download the free ntfy app — Android (Play Store) or iOS (App Store)
2
Make up a unique topic name — something hard to guess like maya-reminders-5821
3
In the ntfy app, tap + and subscribe to your topic
4
Paste the same topic name below and hit Save
5
Hit Send Test to confirm it's working — you'll get a notification!
Make it long and unique — anyone who knows this name can send you notifications
⚠️ Keep this tab open
Reminders are checked every 30 seconds while this page is open. For best results, keep Ping pinned as a tab or add it to your home screen. Notifications won't fire when the page is closed — but all reminders are saved locally and will resume when you reopen it.
